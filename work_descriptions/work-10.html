Rocks, I mean; - Stars

Ádám Salomvári and Laura Oyuela

… is a sonic and spatial performance departing from text, soundscapes, spoken words and its physical surrounding. Here, hope is not mere optimism, but a process of relations that must be enacted within each other. The piece works with analogies between black holes and human beings through low frequencies, orbital motion, and stretched time, shaping a field that gathers bodies into one another. The project investigates how perceptual, cognitive, and affective dynamics shape the experience of artistic performance in relation to contemporary conditions of subjectivity, particularly isolation and hyperproductivity. A fragment of the soundscape may be heard in the space throughout the exhibition.
